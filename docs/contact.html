FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship nationwide?

Yes — FTS ships to all 50 states. Custom hat orders may have additional lead time depending on design complexity.

How do I order a custom hat?

Email us at info@ftsfishing.com or call (724) 824-3474 with your species choice, quantity, and any custom design requests.

Is the FTS Angler App free?

Yes — the FTS Angler App is free to download for all community members. Premium league features may have optional upgrade tiers.

How do I become a retail partner?

Contact us via the form above or email info@ftsfishing.com with "Retail Partnership" in the subject line and we'll send you our partner information.

What is PORT technology?

PORT is our patent-pending AI catch measurement system — voice-activated, it identifies your species, measures length, estimates weight, and auto-posts to your profile.

How do I submit a catch to be featured?

Visit our Community page and fill out the "Show Us Your Catch" form. We'll review all submissions and feature standouts on our site and social media.
