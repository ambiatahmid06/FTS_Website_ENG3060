Fishing with the FTS App Gives You an Edge

Whether you’re casting your first line or chasing your next personal best, FTS is built to be as essential as the tackle you carry. Log catches, track weather, climb the ranks, and connect with a community of shore anglers who share tips, stories, and the thrill of the catch.

App Features

FTS will revolutionize the fishing experience, transforming the way you fish from the shore.

Find Your Best Spots

📍 Smart Location Tracking

Take the guesswork out of shore fishing. FTS uses community-driven data and GPS tracking to help you find proven spots, save your own, and build a map of where you fish best. Spend less time searching and more time casting.

  • ✅ User-shared fishing spot database
  • ✅ Community-rated shore access points
  • ✅ GPS pin your own discoveries
  • ✅ Hotspot heatmaps based on real catch data
Locational GPS

Know When to Fish

🌦 Weather & Conditions

Plan your perfect catch with real-time weather tracking. FTS shows how changing conditions affect the bite so you can fish smarter and make every outing count.

  • ✅ Cloud cover & light conditions
  • ✅ Barometric pressure tracking
  • ✅ Wind speed & direction
  • ✅ Water temperature data
  • ✅ Major & minor bite time predictions
  • ✅ Solunar fishing calendar
Weather Tracking

Species Recognition

🎣 Species Recognition AI

Identify your catch instantly with our hands-free, voice-activated smart Species Recognition tool powered by live video. Just say the word, hold your catch up, and let the app do the rest. Your catch is logged automatically, with real-time insights on regulations, limits, and more.

  • ✅ Real-time species recognition
  • ✅ Voice-activated capture (hands-free)
  • ✅ Expanding species knowledge base
  • ✅ Habitat & regulation info per species
AI Species Recognition
AI Tape Measure

Measure Instantly

📏 Tape Measure

Measure your catch instantly with no extra tools needed. Using PORT’s patent-pending AI, just hold your fish up and let the app do the rest. Your measurement is captured in seconds, safely and with minimal handling, then automatically saved to your profile for competitions or to showcase in the community.

  • ✅ Camera-based length measurement
  • ✅ Automatic weight estimation from length
  • ✅ Measurement saved to catch log
  • ✅ Verified for league submissions
